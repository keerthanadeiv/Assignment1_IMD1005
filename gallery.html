A gallery of horses with varying classes, coats types/colours, average heights, average weights, & origins.

Fallabella

Class:

Pony

Coat:

Wide variety, commonly bay & black.

Average Height:

71-86cm | 24-34 inches

Average Weight:

50-80 kg | 110-176 lbs

Origins:

Argentina, South America

Notes:

Fallabellas are the smallest horses recorded!

Marwari

Class:

Gaited

Coat:

All colour, including piebald & skewballed.

Average Height:

130-140 cm | 51-55 inches

Average Weight:

340-365 kg | 750-1000 lbs

Origins:

Marwari, India

Notes:

Their ears point inward naturally!

Suffolk Punch

Class:

Draft

Coat:

Always chestnut.

Average Height:

165-178 cm | 64-68 inches

Average Weight:

900-1000 kg |2000-2200 lbs

Origins:

Suffolk, England

Notes:

They originated in the early 16th century, and are thought to be the oldest breed of heavy/draft horses in England!

Pintabian

Class:

Warm-Blooded

Coat:

Tobiano Coat Pattern

Average Height:

147-157 cm | 58-62 inches

Average Weight:

400-500 kg | 900-1100 lbs

Origins:

Arabian, Middle East*

Notes:

*They are a backcross between pure-bred Arabian horses, and the Tobianos over 7 generations, resulting in 99% Arabian genes.

Eriskay Pony

Class:

Pony

Coat:

Usually gray, but can also be black/bay.

Average Height:

122-137 cm | 48-52 inches

Average Weight:

325-400 kg | 715-882 lbs

Origins:

Eriskay, Scotland

Notes:

Their coats are naturally waterproof & dense coat, allowing them to thrive in the harsh Scottish conditions.
